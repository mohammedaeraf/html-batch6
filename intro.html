Welcome to the world of HTML

HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language

HTML, or HyperText Markup Language, is the foundational language used to create and structure content on the web. It tells the browser how to display text, images, links, and other elements on a webpage. These tags are enclosed in angle brackets and often come in pairs: an opening tag and a closing tag.
